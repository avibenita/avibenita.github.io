True Data Understanding Through Visualization

Creating a visual histogram from a set of numerical data is probably the best way to truly understand its nature.

Histogram-Ultimate is a statistical utility aimed at generating interactive histograms from a range of numerical values located within an Excel workbook. While other packages, even the most advanced ones, are a kind of statistical "calculators", Histogram-Ultimate is a "live" exploratory entity.

Instead of static and quite unexciting outputs, Histogram-Ultimate makes use of sliders and knobs for interactively exploring data. Packed with a wealth of statistical functions, Histogram-Ultimate should fully answer all the needs involved in univariate analysis (the analysis of one variable at a time).

Comprehensive Statistical Arsenal

Histogram-Ultimate packs a wealth of statistical functions designed to fully answer all needs involved in univariate analysis. From basic descriptive statistics to advanced inferential techniques.

📈

Descriptive & Inferential

  • Interactive fully customizable histogram
  • Complete descriptive statistics suite
  • Hypothesis testing (mean/median/std dev)
  • Power calculations with unique visualization
  • Confidence intervals (classical & Bootstrap)
🔬

Advanced Testing

  • Normality tests (4 different methods)
  • Outlier detection (Grubbs, Quartiles, Dixon Q)
  • PP-plot for 8 distribution families
  • Detrended plots & theoretical distributions
  • Kernel density plots

Interactive Features

  • Live data truncation (left/right)

  • Sliders and knobs for real-time control
  • Percentiles visual calculations
  • Complete statistical calculators set
