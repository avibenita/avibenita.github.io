Inserting a title from EzPaste to slides

 

EzPaste enables the insertion in the slide of a title attached to the pasted chart/table. It works for "Add to new created slides" mode only

 

The title is entered or edited for each selected object (chart/table) into the small yellow window below the list

The chart will appear in the relevant slide as a centered title or formatted according to the active PPT layout and design

Tip: you may easily configure the way the title is formatted in the entire presentation through the PowerPoint slide master mechanism (View/Slide master). If the active presentation is previousely formated, all the the new slides added by Ezpaste will be formatted accordingly
