Where it fits

A utility — not the interactive Statistico philosophy UI

EzPaste is different on purpose. Statistico’s core experience is interactive analysis — explore, see, and understand data in place. EzPaste is a complementary Excel add-in utility: it does not replace that philosophy; it removes the friction of moving finished charts and tables into reports, slides, mail, and files when you need production output fast.
New in EzPaste

AI Review before you export

Before pasting to slides, EzPaste can run an advisory review on your selected charts and tables. It suggests captions, flags quality warnings, and spots possible duplicates — without modifying your workbook.

  • Tick the caption suggestions you want applied to the export list
  • Review warnings before they reach PowerPoint or Word
  • Keep full control — advisory only, your Excel file stays unchanged

Destinations

Six outputs from one Excel workbook

EzPaste identifies charts and tables in the active workbook and pastes them — one selection or hundreds at once — to the destination you choose.

PowerPoint

New slides or predefined slide numbers, with positioning, sizing, titles, and linked updates.

Word

Batch or selection paste for documents that need editable or picture-style Excel objects.

PDF

Publish charts and tables straight into PDF deliverables without rebuilding layouts by hand.

HTML

Move visual content into web-ready pages when you need a lightweight publish path.

Picture files

Export selected objects as image files, with picture styles such as shadow and reflection.

Outlook

Send the current selection into mail in one click instead of multi-step copy and paste special.

Ways of working

Batch paste or one-click selection

Batch pasting

EzPaste lists charts and tables from the active file. Preview each item, check what to send, control order, and paste unlimited items at once — ideal for large decks and recurring report packs.

Selection pasting

Already looking at the chart or table you need? Paste the active selection from the EzPaste toolbar in one step — skipping the usual copy → switch app → paste special sequence.
