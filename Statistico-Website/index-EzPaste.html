Ways of working

Batch paste or one-click selection

Export hundreds of charts with one click — no repetitive copy → paste → resize.

Batch pasting

EzPaste lists charts and tables from the active file. Preview each item, check what to send, control order, and paste unlimited items at once — ideal for large decks and recurring report packs.

Selection pasting

Already looking at the chart or table you need? Paste the active selection from the EzPaste toolbar in one step — skipping the usual copy → switch app → paste special sequence.
