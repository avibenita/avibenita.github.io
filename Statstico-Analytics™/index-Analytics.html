Univariate Analysis

Explore single-variable behavior with publication-ready visuals:

  • Moments & Shape — Mean, trimmed mean, variance, skewness, and kurtosis with live annotations.
  • Distribution Fitting — Overlay normal, lognormal, gamma, or custom kernels on histograms and KDEs.
  • Quantiles & Percentiles — Drag handles to obtain conditional statistics and tail probabilities instantly.
  • Outlier Scans — Box plots, Tukey fences, and robust z-scores flagged inline.
  • Normality Diagnostics — Shapiro-Wilk, Anderson-Darling, and QQ-plots integrated with the VB6 dashboard.

Correlations

Quantify association strength and visualize dependence structures:

  • Correlation Matrix — Pearson, Spearman, and Kendall matrices with heatmaps and confidence bands.
  • Partial & Semi-Partial — Control for covariates and export clean reporting tables.
  • Scatter Studio — Brushed scatterplots, LOESS overlays, and interactive labeling.
  • Significance Testing — Exact p-values, Fisher r-to-z intervals, and multiple-comparison adjustments.
  • Data Sync — sendToHost hooks keep InputsXL selections aligned with correlation panels.

Regression

Full multiple-regression studio with diagnostics and scenario modeling:

  • Model Builder — Add/remove predictors, polynomial terms, or interactions with instant refits.
  • Assumption Checks — Residual, leverage, Cook’s distance, and VIF dashboards.
  • What-If Simulator — Slide predictors to visualize fitted response and prediction intervals.
  • Reporting — APA-ready summary tables, variance decomposition, and effect plots.
  • Export — Stream coefficients straight back to VB6 forms or Excel ranges.

Logistic Regression

Purpose-built workflows for binary or ordinal outcomes:

  • Link Functions — Logit, probit, or complementary log-log options with fit comparisons.
  • Classification Metrics — ROC curves, AUC, confusion matrices, and lift charts.
  • Odds Interpretation — Marginal effects explorer and counterfactual calculators.
  • Regularization — Ridge/LASSO toggles for high-dimensional predictors.
  • Probability Surfaces — Dual predictor heatmaps for stakeholder storytelling.

Factor Analysis

Reveal latent constructs and compress dimensionality:

  • EFA Workflow — Scree plots, MAP tests, and parallel analysis to pick factor counts.
  • PCA Mode — Component loadings, communalities, and biplots with live hover details.
  • Rotation Lab — Varimax, Promax, Oblimin, and custom rotations with side-by-side comparisons.
  • Factor Scores — Bartlett and regression scoring pushed to downstream dashboards.
  • Reliability — Cronbach’s α, McDonald’s ω, and split-half estimates per factor.

Independent Comparisons

Classic between-groups hypothesis tests with visual narratives:

  • t-Tests — One-sample, two-sample pooled, and Welch corrections with effect sizes.
  • ANOVA Suite — One-way and factorial layouts plus Tukey, Games-Howell, and Dunnett contrasts.
  • Distribution-Free Options — Mann-Whitney, Kruskal-Wallis, and permutation overlays.
  • Power Overlays — Display achieved power, detectable effect, and sample planning hints.
  • Story-Ready Charts — Gradient violins, beeswarm overlays, and confidence brackets.

Dependent Comparisons

Within-subjects workflows that emphasize change profiles:

  • Paired t & Wilcoxon — Difference distributions, Bland-Altman plots, and agreement stats.
  • Repeated-Measures ANOVA — Sphericity checks, Greenhouse-Geisser corrections, and profile plots.
  • Multivariate Repeated Measures — MANOVA-style contrasts for multichannel outcomes.
  • Timepoint Storyboards — Align trends, annotate interventions, and export movie loops.
  • Sync to VB6 — notifyColumnChanged wires ensure dashboards refresh as soon as data updates.
