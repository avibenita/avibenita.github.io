The Power of 80/20

The Pareto principle states that roughly 80% of effects come from 20% of causes. This fundamental law applies across business: 80% of sales from 20% of customers, 80% of problems from 20% of sources, 80% of profits from 20% of products.

Smart businesses leverage this insight for dramatic profitability improvements by focusing on the most effective areas while eliminating, automating, delegating, or retraining the rest.
