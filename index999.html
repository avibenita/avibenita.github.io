“Detached Finger” Trick – Step-by-Step

Simplified drawings: the long shapes are the fingers of the base hand, and the rounded shape is the fingertip of the other hand.
Step 1 – Show the base hand
Extend your left hand. Bring your right index finger above the middle of the left index.
Step 2 – Bend & hide the right finger
Bend your right index at the middle joint and tuck the lower part behind the base hand. Only the fingertip should be visible.
Step 3 – Align fingertip with the joint
Place the visible fingertip right over the middle joint of the left index. The audience assumes this tip belongs to that finger.
Step 4 – Slide to create the illusion
Gently move the fingertip left and right. With the connection hidden, the motion looks like the finger is sliding or dislocating in an impossible way.
