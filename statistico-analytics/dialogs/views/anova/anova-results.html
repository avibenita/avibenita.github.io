Assumption Guide
Normality
ANOVA is robust to mild non-normality when group sizes are ≥ 15. For small groups use Kruskal-Wallis.
Homogeneity of variance
Levene p < 0.05 suggests unequal variances. Consider Welch's ANOVA (shown in ANOVA table) or Games-Howell post-hoc.
Independence
Observations must be independent. For repeated/matched measures use the Repeated Measures design.
