Homogeneity of Variance
Assumption Checklist
Normality
ANOVA is robust to mild non-normality when n ≥ 15 per group. For small or heavily skewed groups use Kruskal-Wallis (Results → Non-parametric).
Homogeneity of variance
If Levene p < .05, prefer Welch's ANOVA (Results → Robust) and Games-Howell for post-hoc comparisons.
Independence
Observations must be independent across groups. For within-subject designs use the Repeated Measures design type.
