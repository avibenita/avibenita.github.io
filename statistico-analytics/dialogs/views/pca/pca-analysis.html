Distance in PC Space
The statistic is the squared Mahalanobis distance in the space of retained components (each score squared, weighted by its component’s eigenvalue). Larger values mean an observation is farther from the centre of the data. Use as a visual guide; formal cut-offs compare against χ² quantiles (Hotelling’s T²).
