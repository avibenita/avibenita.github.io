Manual steps in Chrome

  1. Look at the right side of the address bar and click Open in app if it appears.
  2. If it does not appear, open Chrome menu (⋮).
  3. Open Cast, save, and share and look for Install page as app or Install app.
  4. After install, open Statistico Calculator Hub from your apps list.
